# Rumus KPI dan Profit

## Revenue


`Revenue = Revenue Offline + Revenue Online`

## Gross Profit Offline Selling

`Gross Profit Offline Selling = total penjualan Offline`

Keterangan:

`HPP Offline = total harga pokok produk yang terjual melalui offline | hpp_calculations.total_hpp`

## Gross Profit Online Selling

`Gross Profit Online Selling = total penjualan online`

Keterangan:

`HPP Online = total harga pokok produk yang terjual melalui online | hpp_calculations.total_hpp`

## Net Profit Offline Selling

`Net Profit Offline Selling = Gross Profit Offline Selling - HPP Offline`

## Net Profit Online Selling

`Net Profit Online Selling = Gross Profit Online Selling - HPP Online`

## Net Profit

`Net Profit = Net Profit Offline Selling + Net Profit Online Selling`

## NPM

`NPM (Net Profit Margin) = (Net Profit(Net Profit Offline Selling + Net Profit Online Selling) / Revenue(Revenue Offline + Revenue Online)) x 100`

Keterangan:

`NPM menunjukkan persentase laba bersih terhadap total revenue`

## Ringkasan Hubungan Rumus

`Gross Profit -> Revenue -> Net Profit -> NPM`

Detail:

`Gross Profit = Revenue`

`Net Profit = Gross Profit - Hpp`

`NPM = (Net Profit(Net Profit Offline Selling + Net Profit Online Selling) / Revenue(Revenue Offline + Revenue Online)) x 100`
